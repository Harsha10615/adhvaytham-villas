COMMUNITY LIFE

Designed for Your Family's Comfort

Enjoy a rich and diverse social lifestyle. Our world-class amenities are fully completed, beautifully maintained, and available for use by all current residents.

Grand Clubhouse

A multi-story 15,000 sq. ft. community center equipped with a fitness gym, card room, table tennis tables, library lounge, and administrative desk.

Children's Play Area

Dedicated sandpit and rubberized turf fields with high-quality swings, slides, climbing frames, and bench seating for watching parents.

Parks and Gardens

Beautifully manicured botanical paths, walking trails, gazebo structures, and specialized open yoga/meditation platforms.

Multi-Tier Security

Guarded main gate entrance, high-definition peripheral CCTV surveillance, RFID-enabled car access system, and intercom connectivity.

Swimming Pool

A full-size rectangular swimming pool with clean filtration systems, along with a separate shallow pool for kids and outdoor shower rooms.

Peripheral Walking Track

A 1.5 km long, safe, asphalt-paved pedestrian loop lined with deciduous trees and streetlights, designed for jogging and seniors' walks.

100% Power Backup

Continuous electricity assurance with high-capacity silent diesel generators powering common streetlights, pumps, and individual villas.

Indoor Sports Arena

Enjoy indoor games in the clubhouse including professional billiards tables, chess tables, carrom boards, and table tennis tables.

Reliable Water Systems

Continuous municipal water line connection supplemented by deep borewells, connected to a central underground storage sump and water softener unit.

COMMUNITY ECOSYSTEM

An Eco-Friendly, Smart Gated Community

We believe in building sustainable real estate projects. Adhvaytham Villas features green spaces and resource-saving infrastructure that reduces long-term maintenance costs for residents.

  • Rainwater harvesting systems across all villa units
  • Solar-powered lighting for pathways and peripheral fences
  • Sewage treatment plant (STP) with treated water utilized for gardens
