COMMON QUERIES

Everything You Need to Know

Have questions about the project, possession status, or banking clearances? Find answers to our most frequently asked queries below.

Is the project occupied by residents?

Yes, Adhvaytham Villas is a fully active, functioning residential neighborhood. Phase 1 has been successfully delivered and over 45 families are currently residing inside completed villas. Standard facilities such as security gates, internal roadways, and power backups are fully operational.

Are ready-to-move villas available for purchase?

Yes, we have a very limited number of ready-to-move-in villas available in our Phase 1 configuration (predominantly 3BHK Executive and 4BHK Signature options). Please reach out to our sales desk to check real-time availability and pricing metrics.

Which phase is currently under construction?

Phase 2 (consisting of 25 villas) is currently under active construction. The RCC structural frames are completed, and plastering, brick partitioning, and line fittings are in progress. Phase 2 units are scheduled for key handovers starting in late 2026. Phase 3 excavation works are also under prep.

Is home loan assistance available?

Yes, the project is approved by major nationalized and private banks (including SBI, HDFC, ICICI, and LIC Housing Finance). Our in-house sales team will assist you with loan eligibility checks, document compilations, and bank processing approvals.

Is the project RERA approved?

Yes, Adhvaytham Villas is completely RERA compliant. All development phases are registered under RERA, and layout documents, structural designs, and RERA progress cards are transparently accessible for verification.

What are the water supply and electrical arrangements?

The community features a dual water system: primary supply via municipal water connections combined with multiple deep borewells for backups, stored in a central underground storage sump. Electricity is supplied by the local electricity board, backed by automatic heavy-duty generator units.

Have More Questions for Us?

Our sales office is open 7 days a week. Speak directly to our legal and real-estate advisors for clear information.
